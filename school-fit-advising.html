üìã Our Advising Process

1

Initial Assessment

We begin with a comprehensive evaluation of your athletic profile, academic interests, and personal preferences.

  • Athletic performance metrics and potential
  • Academic record and intended major
  • Geographic preferences and campus culture
  • Financial considerations and scholarship opportunities
2

School Research & Analysis

Our advisors research and analyze potential schools based on your profile, considering multiple factors for the best fit.

  • Athletic program strength and competition level
  • Academic reputation and program offerings
  • Campus culture and student life
  • Graduation rates and career outcomes
  • Financial aid and scholarship availability
3

Personalized Recommendations

We provide a curated list of schools that best match your profile, with detailed explanations of why each school is a good fit.

  • Top matches with highest compatibility scores
  • Reach schools that challenge your potential
  • Safety schools that ensure options
  • Detailed pros and cons for each recommendation
4

Recruitment Strategy

We help you develop a strategic approach to connecting with your target schools and maximizing your chances of acceptance.

  • Timeline planning for recruitment process
  • Communication strategies with coaches and admissions
  • Highlight reel and athletic resume development
  • Interview preparation and campus visit planning
5

Ongoing Support

We provide continued guidance throughout your college application and decision-making process.

  • Application review and feedback
  • Scholarship negotiation assistance
  • Decision-making support when offers arrive
  • Transition planning for your chosen school
