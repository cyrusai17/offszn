NIL Training Course

Master the fundamentals of Name, Image, and Likeness (NIL) opportunities to maximize your earning potential as a student-athlete.

UNIVERSITY PARTNER

This course is offered in partnership with university partners to ensure compliance with NCAA regulations and institutional policies.

Who Is This Course For?

Our NIL Training Course is designed for:

  • Current NCAA student-athletes looking to understand and capitalize on NIL opportunities
  • High school athletes preparing for college athletics
  • Student-athletes seeking to build their personal brand
  • Athletes interested in entrepreneurship and business development
  • Coaches and athletic staff supporting student-athletes in NIL endeavors

Course Outline

Module 1: NIL Fundamentals

Understanding the basics of Name, Image, and Likeness

  • History and evolution of NIL rights
  • Current NCAA regulations and compliance requirements
  • Institutional policies and restrictions
  • State-by-state NIL legislation overview

Module 2: Personal Branding

Building a strong personal brand as a student-athlete

  • Developing your unique value proposition
  • Social media strategy and best practices
  • Content creation and storytelling
  • Building an engaged audience

Module 3: Business Development

Turning your brand into business opportunities

  • Identifying potential brand partnerships
  • Negotiating contracts and agreements
  • Setting appropriate rates and fees
  • Creating a business plan for NIL activities

Module 4: Financial Management

Managing your NIL earnings responsibly

  • Tax implications and reporting requirements
  • Budgeting and financial planning
  • Investment strategies for student-athletes
  • Working with financial advisors

Module 5: Legal Considerations

Understanding the legal landscape of NIL

  • Contract review and negotiation
  • Intellectual property rights
  • Working with agents and representatives
  • Compliance monitoring and reporting

Key Takeaways

Comprehensive NIL Knowledge

Gain a thorough understanding of NIL regulations, opportunities, and best practices to make informed decisions about your athletic career.

Personal Brand Development

Learn how to build and maintain a strong personal brand that attracts opportunities and resonates with your target audience.

Business Acumen

Develop essential business skills including negotiation, contract review, and financial management to maximize your earning potential.

Compliance Confidence

Understand how to navigate the complex regulatory landscape of NIL while maintaining eligibility and avoiding violations.

Long-term Career Planning

Learn how to leverage NIL opportunities to build a foundation for your post-athletic career in business, media, or entrepreneurship.
