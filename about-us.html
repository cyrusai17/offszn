About OFFSZN

Empowering student-athletes to maximize their potential both on and off the field.

🎯 Our Vision

At OFFSZN, we envision a world where student-athletes have access to the resources, guidance, and opportunities they need to excel in their athletic careers and beyond. We believe that the skills, discipline, and determination developed through sports can translate into success in all areas of life.

Our mission is to bridge the gap between athletic talent and professional success by providing comprehensive support services tailored specifically for student-athletes. We aim to be the trusted partner that helps athletes navigate the complex landscape of collegiate sports, transfer opportunities, personal branding, and career development.

📖 Our Story

OFFSZN was born in the spring of 2025 at Tulane University, when a group of undergraduate student-athletes recognized a significant gap in the support available to their peers. These founders, who had experienced firsthand the challenges of balancing athletics, academics, and personal development, came together with a shared vision: to create a platform that would empower student-athletes to make informed decisions about their future.

What began as a small project to help teammates navigate transfer deadlines quickly evolved into a comprehensive support system. The founders spent countless hours researching NCAA regulations, consulting with athletic advisors, and interviewing fellow athletes to understand their needs. They discovered that many student-athletes lacked access to reliable information about transfer opportunities, NIL regulations, and career planning resources.

With the support of Tulane's entrepreneurship program and mentorship from experienced professionals in the sports industry, OFFSZN was officially launched. The platform has since grown to serve student-athletes across the country, providing them with the tools and guidance they need to succeed both on and off the field.
