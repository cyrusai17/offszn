Financial & Career Planning

Expert guidance on managing finances as a student athlete and planning for your professional career path after graduation.

💰 About Financial & Career Planning

Our Financial & Career Planning service helps student-athletes navigate the unique financial challenges they face and develop a strategic approach to their professional future. We combine financial expertise with career development strategies to ensure you're prepared for success both during and after your athletic career.

What We Offer

  • Personalized financial planning tailored to student-athlete needs
  • NIL income management and investment strategies
  • Career development planning and skill assessment
  • Industry connections and networking opportunities
  • Transition planning from athletics to professional career

👥 Who Is This For?

Current Student-Athletes

Looking to manage NIL earnings, scholarships, and prepare for financial independence while balancing athletic and academic commitments.

Graduating Athletes

Planning for the transition from collegiate athletics to professional life, whether in sports or another industry.

Professional Athletes

Seeking guidance on managing income, investments, and planning for life after their athletic career.

Parents & Guardians

Supporting their student-athletes through financial decisions and career planning processes.

📋 Our Planning Process

1

Financial Assessment

We begin with a comprehensive evaluation of your current financial situation and future goals.

  • Income sources (NIL, scholarships, part-time work)
  • Current expenses and budgeting needs
  • Financial goals and priorities
  • Risk tolerance and investment preferences
2

Financial Strategy Development

We create a personalized financial plan that addresses your unique needs as a student-athlete.

  • Budgeting strategies for athletic lifestyle
  • Tax planning for NIL income
  • Savings and investment recommendations
  • Debt management and credit building
  • Insurance and risk protection
3

Career Assessment

We evaluate your skills, interests, and career aspirations to identify potential pathways.

  • Skills inventory and strengths assessment
  • Career interest exploration
  • Industry research and opportunity identification
  • Educational and certification planning
4

Career Strategy Development

We help you create a strategic plan for your professional development and career transition.

  • Career goal setting and timeline development
  • Resume and LinkedIn profile optimization
  • Networking strategy and relationship building
  • Interview preparation and job search tactics
  • Professional branding and personal marketing
5

Implementation & Ongoing Support

We provide guidance on implementing your plans and offer continued support as your situation evolves.

  • Action plan development with specific next steps
  • Regular check-ins and plan adjustments
  • Resource recommendations and referrals
  • Crisis management for unexpected financial or career challenges
