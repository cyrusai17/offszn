Yes - NCAA Transfer Portal

Process: Athletes must enter the portal during designated windows based on sport and season.

Portal Access Rules

  • Athletes must request entry via written notification to their school.
  • Schools have 2 business days to submit athlete info to the portal.
  • Athletes can commit any time, but must enter during the window.

‚öñÔ∏è Division II: Notification-Based Process

Portal Access

Yes

Process: No sport-specific windows; governed by a single notification deadline

Key Rules

  • Must submit written notification of transfer by June 15 of each academic year.
  • Once notification is submitted:
    • The school has 7 days to enter the athlete into the portal.
    • Athletes must complete an educational module before entry.
  • Athletes may practice and compete at the new school the next year, assuming all eligibility standards are met.
